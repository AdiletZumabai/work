Smart Watch

Timekeeping

Of course, smartwatches tell time accurately and can often be customized with various watch faces to suit your style.

Fitness Tracking

Many smartwatches have built-in sensors to monitor your activity, such as step counting, distance traveled, calories burned, and heart rate tracking. Some even have GPS for accurate location tracking during outdoor activities.

Payments

Some smartwatches are equipped with NFC technology, allowing you to make contactless payments using services like Apple Pay or Google Pay.
