Laptops

Portability

One of the defining features of a laptop is its portability. Unlike desktop computers, laptops are designed to be carried easily.

Connectivity

Laptops offer a range of connectivity options, including USB ports, HDMI, audio jacks, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and, in some cases, cellular connectivity for internet access on the go.

Battery Life

Laptops feature batteries that can last from a few hours to an entire workday, depending on usage and battery capacity.
